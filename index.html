FAQ

Answers before you ask.

Is my money safe? Do you hold funds?+

No. AlphaEdge is a signal terminal — we never see or touch your Polymarket wallet. You place trades manually on Polymarket using the signal direction. Your keys, your funds, always.

How fast is the data?+

BTC price and L2 orderbook stream directly from Binance WebSocket (sub-second). Polymarket prices refresh every 30 seconds from the official Gamma + CLOB APIs. No cached, stale feeds.

What's the actual win rate?+

Across locked signals (conf ≥ 58%, 8/10 persist) over the last 30 days: 77.4%. Equity curve on this page updates daily. Note: past performance ≠ future results. Always paper-trade first.

Do I need a Polymarket account?+

Yes — AlphaEdge is the signal, Polymarket is the execution venue. Set up a Polymarket wallet with USDC on Polygon first, then use AlphaEdge to decide UP or DOWN each window.

Why lifetime and not monthly?+

One-time payment aligns incentives — we win when the product works long-term, not when we churn you through recurring billing. Existing customers are always grandfathered into new features.

What if I don't want to trade immediately?+

Every signal is visible in the dashboard whether you trade it or not. Paper-trade alongside for a few sessions to verify the edge on your screen before risking real capital.

Can I run it on mobile?+

Yes — the dashboard is fully responsive. Most traders use desktop during active sessions and mobile as a monitor when away.

How do I get support?+

Telegram: @AlphaEdgeDev or @justinthedeev. Usually respond within 1–2 hours. Discord community launch Q2 2026.
